About me

I am a freelance consultant, manager and developer born in Belgrade, Serbia. Currently, I am studying master program in Computer Science on the EPFL in Lausanne Switzerland. I have a professional background in project management, and a bachelor degree in Organizational Sciences.

Whether your next project is an online presence, market research, or a simple event, I'll be ready to help!

This is what I do

I provide project and product management, marketing consulting and mobile development services.

Project management

Taking the project from single idea, through planning phase and different activities, to the realization phase, reaching project goals. Creating cross-functional communication, leading teams of people from different sectors.

Marketing consulting

Services of online and offline promotions, maintaing or improving the brand, market analysis and public relations.

Product management

Creating the public image of the product, establishing market position, introducing new products, or improving the global position of the current ones.

Mobile app development

Development of mobile phone applications, specially for the Windows Phone environment.

Work hard, don't be afraid of the change, accept challenging positions, communication and networking, continuous learning for innovative areas.
